SGIP Level 1 Pathway
As laid out in 20VAC5-314-40 of the Virginia Administrative Code:

"B. The IC shall submit (i) a complete Level 1 Interconnection Request Form (Schedule 5 of 20VAC5-314-170); (ii) site control documentation pursuant to Schedule 5 of 20VAC5-314-170; and (iii) the required $100 processing fee to the utility. Alternatively, the utility may require use of a commission-approved Interconnection Request Form similar to Schedule 5 of 20VAC5-314-170, which shall be made available to customers on the utility's website."

The utility shall then notify the IC of any defficiences and necessary modifications. Once any required modifications or additions (As provided for in 20VAC5-314-40-C) and the additional requirements in 20VAC5-314-40-D are completed, the IC may begin operation of an SGF.
