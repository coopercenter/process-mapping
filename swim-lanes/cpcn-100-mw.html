Federal Reviews
Depending on project characteristics, federal reviews may be required from any number of federal agencies, including the U.S. Bureau of Land Management, U.S. Forest Service, U.S. Bureau of Indian Affairs, Federal Aviation Administration, and the U.S. Department of Defense. Two of the more common federal reviews required are from U.S. Army Corps of Engineers and U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, outlined here.
